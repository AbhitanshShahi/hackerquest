About Sahayata

A real-time, community-based platform for local aid.

Sahayata (which means "help" or "aid" in Hindi) is a real-time, community-based platform designed to connect those with surplus resources to those who need them. It's built on the principle of local, peer-to-peer assistance, aiming to create a stronger, more supportive neighborhood.

The portal is organized into key sections for different types of resources, including food, medical supplies, education materials, and household items. The goal is to facilitate mutual aid and reduce waste within a community by providing an intuitive platform for giving and receiving.

Key Features

  • Real-Time Listings: See what's available for donation in your local area, updated instantly.
  • AI-Powered Image Scan: Use generative AI to automatically identify and categorize household items from a picture, simplifying the donation process.
  • Secure Authentication: Log in securely using email or Google to manage your donations and requests.
  • Category-Based Listings: Find and offer help in specific categories like Food, Medical, and Education.
